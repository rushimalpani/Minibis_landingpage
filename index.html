App Features

Order History and Tracking

Customers can access their order history and track the status of their purchases, enhancing transparency and trust in the buying process.

Eliminates Social Media Order Scattering

Brand owners can efficiently sell their products through MiniBis, eliminating the need to manage orders from various social media platforms like Instagram, WhatsApp

User-Friendly Android Interface

MiniBis is built exclusively for Android, ensuring a familiar and easy-to-use platform for both sellers and customers.

Local Business Promotion

MiniBis supports and promotes Indian small businesses. This feature encourages community support and economic growth by prioritizing local entrepreneurs.

About Us

MiniBis is an E-Retail App designed for small businesses, exclusively on Android for user-friendliness. Leveraging the widespread familiarity with Android, it empowers brand owners to sell products efficiently, steering them away from scattered social media orders. The app streamlines customer data, product management, and secure payment options, reducing online fraud risks. Its low-maintenance design simplifies business operations.
